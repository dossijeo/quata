## Full description

Qüata is a social app for connecting with people, neighborhoods and communities through posts, conversations and multimedia content.

Explore a visual feed with text, image and video posts. Join conversations with comments, likes and shares, follow profiles and discover nearby or interest-based communities. Qüata also includes real-time messaging, notifications, shared files and safety options such as configurable SOS contacts.

The app is built for a smooth mobile experience: browse anonymously, then sign in to use social actions and private features. It includes multimedia creation tools, image and video editing, on-demand automatic captions, translation for chats and comments, and interface support for English, Spanish and French.

Highlights:

- Visual feed with text, image and video posts.
- Real-time chats and conversations.
- Communities and neighborhoods inside Qüata.
- Profiles, following and shared files.
- Image and video editing with automatic captions.
- Text translation between languages.
- SOS contacts and notifications.
- Localized experience in English, Spanish and French.

Qüata brings community, communication and multimedia into one mobile space.
